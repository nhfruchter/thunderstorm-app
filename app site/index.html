Thunderstorm

A simple, glanceable weather app using the best info.

Features

Get clear, accurate weather information when you need it.

  • Up to the minute temperatures and forecasts, along with the great hourly and daily forecasts you need to plan your day or week.
  • Looking stormy outside? Stuck at the bus stop? If it's going to rain, find out when it's going to start and stop—down to the minute.
  • View severe weather warnings and alerts.
  • Backed by some of the best data out there, provided by Forecast.io.
